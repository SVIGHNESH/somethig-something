# User Guide

Welcome to the CoFounders orchestration UI! This guide will explain how to use the web interface to convert a PRD into an AI-executed project.

## Overview
The application follows a 4-step wizard:
1.  **Define Requirements:** Upload your PRD.
2.  **Assemble Team:** Upload your employees' resumes.
3.  **Generate Crew:** The AI matches tasks to people and configures the agents.
4.  **Execute:** Watch the agents build the project in real-time.

---

## Step 1: Define Requirements (PRD)
The system needs to know what you want to build.

1.  Navigate to **Step 1: PRD** in the sidebar.
2.  Choose your input method using the tabs at the top:
    *   **Text Mode:** Paste your plain text PRD into the text area.
    *   **File Mode:** Drag and drop a `.txt` or `.pdf` file into the designated dropzone.
3.  Click the **Parse Requirements** button.
4.  Wait a few seconds. The system will extract the requirements into a list of specific, actionable tasks, displaying them on the right side of the screen.

---

## Step 2: Assemble Team
Now you need to tell the system who is available to work on the project.

1.  Navigate to **Step 2: Team** in the sidebar.
2.  Drag and drop your team members' resumes (must be `.pdf` format) into the upload box. You can do this one by one or select multiple files.
3.  As each resume is uploaded, you will see a loading skeleton card.
4.  Once parsed, a card will appear for the employee showing:
    *   Their extracted Name and Contact Info.
    *   Their Experience level (Fresher vs. Experienced).
    *   A list of their Technical Skills (extracted automatically via NLP).
5.  If you upload a resume by mistake, click the **X** in the top right of the card to remove them.

---

## Step 3: Generate Crew & Review Assignments
This is where the magic happens. The AI will look at the tasks from Step 1 and the skills from Step 2 to make assignments.

1.  Navigate to **Step 3: Crew** in the sidebar.
2.  Ensure that the prerequisites are met (green checkmarks indicating you have parsed a PRD and uploaded at least one resume).
3.  Click **Generate Crew Setup**.
4.  **Review the Output:**
    *   **Code Preview:** View the generated `agents.yaml` and `tasks.yaml` files.
    *   **Assignment Board:** Scroll down to view the visual Kanban-style board. You will see columns for each employee, showing the specific tasks assigned to them based on their skills.
    *   **Gap Identification:** If a task required a skill your team didn't have (e.g., "Kubernetes"), the system will automatically create a new agent persona to handle it. These "New Hires" will be highlighted at the top of the page.

---

## Step 4: Execute & Monitor
With the crew configured, it's time to put them to work.

1.  Navigate to **Step 4: Execute** in the sidebar.
2.  Click **Run Crew in Docker**.
3.  A terminal window will appear showing real-time streaming logs. You will see:
    *   The Docker container building.
    *   Agents waking up and taking action.
    *   Tools being used (e.g., file reading, web searching).
    *   Final outputs being generated.
4.  Once the run is marked as `Completed ✓`, a **Download Workspace Zip** button will appear. Click it to download all the code and text files generated by the AI agents.

---

## Troubleshooting
*   **"Only .txt or .pdf files are supported."** - Ensure your PRD is in the correct format. Word documents (`.docx`) are not currently supported.
*   **"Resume upload failed."** - Ensure the PDF is not password protected or corrupted.
*   **Infinite Loading Spinner:** Check your terminal where the backend is running. If Docker is not running on your machine, Step 4 will fail.
